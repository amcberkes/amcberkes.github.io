Hey there! I am currently pursuing a PhD in Prof. Srinivasan Keshavs’s Energy & Environment Group in the Department of Computer Science & Technology at the University of Cambridge. My research focuses on the decarbonistion of buildings using Machine Learning and is funded by the Gates Cambridge Trust. My past research has been in the field of designing and operating sustainable energy systems in residential homes, that include solar PV, stationary batteries and bidirectional EVs. I have built a novel algorithm that takes a personalised approach for the joint sizing and operation of solar PV and storage for single-family homes (SOPEVS). I have also built a synthetic data generation tool for EV usage traces (SPAGHETTI). My current research interest lies in data-driven retrofit and operation strategies for commercial buildings. I previously studied Computer Science at ETH Zürich.
